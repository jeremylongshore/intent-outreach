Guidance for reading ENRICHMENT signals when scoring fit and choosing outreach angles.

Enrichment can include:
- `funding`: lastRound, totalRaisedUsd, lastRoundDate, investors. Recent funding (last ~12 months) is a
  strong "buying now" signal — new budget, new hires, new initiatives. Old or absent funding is weaker.
- `verifiedEmail` / `phone`: a deliverability signal, not a buying signal.
- `data.webContext`: recent web results (titles + urls). Use these for timely, specific angles (a product
  launch, a hire, a press mention) — but only if the result is clearly about THIS company and recent.

How to use it:
- Prefer the most specific, most recent, most clearly-attributable signal.
- A single concrete signal beats three vague ones.
- If enrichment is empty or stale, do not manufacture urgency — score on fundamentals (industry, size)
  and keep angles generic-but-honest.
- Never treat an unverified web snippet as established fact in the outreach.
